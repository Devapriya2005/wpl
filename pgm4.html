WELCOME TO MITS COLLEGE



Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science(MITS) is a private engineering college in Kochi, Kerala, founded in 2013 and promoted by the Muthoot Group. Affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by the AICTE, it offers various undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs, including popular specializations in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Cyber Security, with NBA accreditation for several programs. MITS provides state-of-the-art infrastructure and industry interface opportunities, aiming to be a center of excellence focused on meritocracy and the holistic development of students.

Vision

Be the most trusted, globally diversified institution enriching lives of the masses while contributing back to the society.

Mission

To build leading customer-centric businesses enabled by technology, maintaining the highest standards of corporate governance and uncompromising values.
